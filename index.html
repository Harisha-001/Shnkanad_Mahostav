About Sanatan Sanstha, the Organiser of the Sanatan Rashtra Shankhnad Mahotsav

To facilitate the establishment of Sanatan Rashtra (Ram Rajya) on Earth, Sachchidananda Parabrahman Dr Athavale created the platform of Sanatan Sanstha (rooted in Sanatan Dharma) to prepare society to herald in this Divine era. For over 25 years, Sachchidananda Parabrahman Dr Athavale has been guiding society, nurturing seekers and leading spiritual initiatives across Bharat. Those who wanted to grow spiritually were provided with clear principles and guidelines to nurture their spiritual journey. Every effort was made to assist them in their spiritual growth. In response to the present challenges confronting Bharat and with a clear vision for the protection and propagation of Sanatan Dharma for future generations, Sachchidananda Parabrahman Dr Athavale strengthened the bond and unity between different organisations working for Sanatan Dharma.

The Significance of the Name of the event- Why Shankhnad?

In our scriptures, 'Shankhnad' (blowing of the conch) symbolizes valor and auspicious beginnings. Hence, blowing the conch of Sanatan Rashtra Shankhnad Mahotsav signifies that the mission behind it will be an essential stepping stone towards societal unity and harmony. It will be like a trumpet call that will resonate in schools, it heralds unity amongst Hindu sects, cultural revival, and the protection of Sanatan Dharma for future generations.
